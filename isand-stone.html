MINT

This tough and high quality sandstone exudes an old-world, rustic charm that has fans in India and over the world. Its intricate patterns and ultra-fine background renders it perfect for both residential and commercial purposes.

TEAK

With rich colour striations running across the stone and sandy yellow surface in the background, this is popularly used for kitchen countertops, vanity tops, table tops, wall cladding, wall capping, fountain, flooring, etc.

RAINBOW

RAINBOW

Resembling the vast stretches of sand layered in a desert area, this stone variety is used for exterior and interior construction and design purposes. Also known as Khatu Rainbow, this finds favour because of its sturdy nature and colour fastness.
