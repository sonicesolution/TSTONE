RAJASTHAN BLACK


Known for its incredible shine, long durability and rich colour, Rajasthan Black is widely used in countertops, table tops, kitchen tops and flooring.

STEEL GREY


Offer your bathroom, countertop, staircase and kitchen top a chic look using this durable granite. Steel grey, with its metallic shine and mosaic of grey tones enhances the look of any home, hotel or resort.

BLACK GALAXY

TIGER SKIN
