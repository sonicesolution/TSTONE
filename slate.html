SILVER-GREY


Plan a sleek and sophisticated look for your home or office space with this modish looking, pollution-resistant, frost-resistant stone variety. Also known as the Silver Grey Quartzite, it is a popular choice for wall cladding, flooring, countertops and paving.

ZEERA-GREEN


Perfect for interiors, exteriors and apparently every other design project, Zeera Green is massively recognised for its rot-proof, waterproof and hard-wearing nature. It is widely used for masonry, pavers, monuments, pool coping, sills, etc.

SILVER SHINE


Also known as Silver Shine Quartzite, this slate stone adds a beautiful sheen to any structure or construction in flooring, countertops, wall cladding or paving.

DEOLI GREEN


The distinguished colour variation of Deoli Green accentuates the look of every structure where used. Its shiny glaze renders it ideal for placement in the lobby, parking, dining area, temple room, kitchen and many other locations.

OCEAN


Captivate your onlookers with the unbeatable shine of the Ocean slate variety that is perfect for interior and exterior decor and flooring purposes.

MULTI


Reflecting impeccable hues in bright sunlight or wall lights, the natural make and fashion of this multipurpose stone are what makes it special. It’s availability in a multitude of tones render it ideal for both rural and urban setting.

COPPER


One of the most widely used slate varieties for interiors and exteriors of high-end places, the copper slate is recognized for its incredible shine. It finds extensive use in building construction, wall claddings, roofing, fireplace, pools, etc.

PEACOCK


Brighten up the appearance of your home or office space with Peacock slate that dresses your floors and walls with impeccable hues like deep purples, dark blues, coppers, and reds.

JACK BLACK


The blackish hues of this stone variety offer a sophisticated look. Stand a class apart with the magnificent Jack Black adorning your living space, home walls or lobby area.
