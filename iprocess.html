Mining

It begins with the extraction of the stone blocks from the quarry. For a unique and enchanting look, these are often extracted along with the "vein" of the Deposit.

Sorting

Before the stones could be cut, polished or treated any further, they are sorted to match the quality standards.

Cutting - Hand, machine, Gangsaw, other

The stone varieties are cut and given a new, more improved finish maintaining industry standards for a final smooth and lustrous look and feel.

Packaging

The final product, as requested is packaged with great care to prevent any damage to the stone slabs.

Delivery

Quality offered, on time, every time.

Loading

Product care ensured at every step.
